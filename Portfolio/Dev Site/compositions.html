Compositions

Below is a list of works that I composed.

  • Symphony No. 1 - For Jean

    Instrumentation: Symphony Orchestra, with euphonium.
    Symphony No. 1 - For Jean is a single-movement symphony. It is inspired by Jean Sibelius' symphonic works, particularly his Sixth and Seventh symphonies. Like Sibelius' Seventh, the form of this piece is four-in-one. It progresses from tension and obscurity to clarity and triumph. The dedication is two-fold: the inspiration of Sibelius, and dedication to my grandmother, Jean. The symphony is based on her favorite hymn, "How Great Thou Art".
    Duration: 15 minutes

  • Tiny Steps - An Awkward Dance for Tuba Quartet

    Instrumentation: 2 euphonium, 2 tuba
    Tiny Steps is a tongue-in-cheek piece, meant to challenge big instruments. The title is a play on Coltrane's Giant Steps, and instructions for the limitation that I followed when writing. In the first half of the piece, each player can only change pitch by a half-step at a time. For the second half, the limitation changes by a half-step, which makes the players only able to change pitch by whole-steps.
    Selected for performance at the Open Space Festival of New Music, 2015.
    Duration: 5 minutes

  • Quintet for Winds

    Instrumentation: Flute, Clarinet, Alto Saxophone, Bassoon, Tuba
    Quintet for Winds was conceived as a project for my friends at Ball State University, resulting in an atypical ensemble. The form is a modified rondo, where the primary section is varied on every return.
    Duration: 5 minutes

  • Gourmet

    Instrumentation: Electroacoustic, stereo mix
    Gourmet is my first venture into electronic composition. All the sound material used was recorded in my kitchen. Through processing in Audacity, Logic, and Reaktor, I created the sounds heard in the piece. It is a sectional composition, using a distinct "glass-clink" as the signpost for each new section.
    Duration: 4:38

  • Generic 90's Video Game Music

    Instrumentation: Electronic
    This work is a simple MIDI composition, demonstrating MIDI manipulation using Digital Performer.
    Duration: 2:35

  • Valles Fanfare

    Instrumentation: Brass and Percussion
    Valles Fanfare was written as a submission for the Valles Symphony Orchestra. It is inspired by the music of Andalusian composer Isaac Albeniz.
    Duration: 2 minutes

  • Praise the Prowess of People-Kings

    Instrumentation: Double Trombone Quartet, tenor and bass solos
    Praise the Prowess of People-Kings is a showpiece for trombone choir. It is a tone poem based on the epic Beowulf. Solo tenor trombone represents the hero, while solo bass trombone play the malevolent Grendel.
    Duration: 5 minutes
